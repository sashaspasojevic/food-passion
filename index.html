Best roast leg of lamb

This is without a doubt one of the best ways to cook a leg of lamb. Cooking on a high heat gives you super-crispy potatoes but the lamb is still juicy.

Ingredients :
  • 2 kg leg of lamb or hogget
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 1/2 a bunch of fresh rosemary
  • 1.5 kg potatoes
  • 1 lemon
  • olive oil
Mint Sauce :
  • 1 bunch of fresh mint
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3 tablespoons wine vinegar
